# Wiki Overview

This wiki is the compiled knowledge layer between raw sources and downstream answers.

## Purpose

- preserve synthesized knowledge across sessions
- accumulate structure over time
- make cross-references explicit
- reduce repeated re-reading of raw materials

## Main sections

- `wiki/sources/` — summaries of individual sources
- `wiki/concepts/` — topic and concept pages
- `wiki/entities/` — people, orgs, projects, tools, models
- `wiki/syntheses/` — comparisons, theses, reusable analyses

## Maintenance notes

- Add reusable knowledge here, not only in chat history.
- Prefer updating existing pages before creating new ones.
- Keep links dense enough that Obsidian graph view stays useful.
